Popular Cartoons

Pokémon

Pokémon is an iconic Japanese animated series that began airing in 1997. The show follows the adventures of Ash Ketchum, a young Pokémon Trainer, and his loyal companion Pikachu as they travel through various regions, aiming to become a Pokémon Master. by capturing and training Pokémon (creatures with special powers). Along the way, they encounter friends, rivals, and the villainous Team Rocket, who are constantly trying to steal rare Pokémon. The Pokémon universe includes a wide array of species, each with unique abilities, and the series emphasizes themes of friendship, perseverance, and the bond between Pokémon and their trainers. With its engaging battles, colorful world, and memorable characters, Pokémon became a global phenomenon, spawning movies, trading cards, video games, and a lasting legacy in pop culture. You can watch Pokémon on platforms like Netflix, Hulu, and the official Pokémon TV website.

Courage the Cowardly Dog

Courage the Cowardly Dog is an animated series created by John R. Dilworth that aired on Cartoon Network from 1996 to 2002. It follows Courage, a timid pink dog living with his elderly owners, Muriel and Eustace Bagge. Despite his fear of supernatural threats, Courage often bravely protects his owners from bizarre creatures and eerie events that invade their lives. The show blends horror and comedy, with Courage facing a new monster or antagonist in each episode. Its unique animation style, combining dark and whimsical elements, has garnered a cult following, making it a beloved classic in animated television history. You can watch Courage the Cowardly Dog on HBO Max, Amazon Prime Video, and on the official Cartoon Network website.

SpongeBob SquarePants

Spongebob is one of those shows you've inevitably seen if you have a TV. Spongebob premiered in 1999 and was created by Stephen Hillenburg. The show focuses on the life of a sponge who lives in a pineapple in the middle of the ocean, along with his 2 neighbors. His dimwitted starfish friend Patrick lives under a rock, while Squidward, an irritable squid, lives in a tiki head. SpongeBob goes to work at the Krusty Krab, serving burgers called Krabby Patties, with Squidward as the cashier. Their greedy boss, Mr. Krabs, does whatever it takes to make money, while Plankton constantly tries to steal the Krabby Patty formula. You can watch SpongeBob SquarePants on Nick, Amazon Prime Video, and on Youtube.

Top 10 Cartoons of the 90s

Discover the top 10 cartoons from the 90s and what made them so special!

Here are some of the most beloved cartoons of the 90s:

  • Animaniacs:
    A zany series featuring the Warner siblings, who entertain with their comedic antics and catchy songs.
  • Rugrats:
    Follow the adventures of a group of toddlers exploring their imaginative world and the challenges they face.
  • The Powerpuff Girls:
    Three super-powered girls, Blossom, Bubbles, and Buttercup, save the day while balancing childhood.
  • Doug:
    Doug Funnie navigates middle school life with daydreams and adventures alongside his friends.
  • Dexter's Laboratory:
    A boy genius with a secret lab must outsmart his sister Dee Dee, who constantly disrupts his experiments.
  • Hey Arnold!:
    Arnold and his friends tackle the ups and downs of urban life with humor and heart.
  • Batman: The Animated Series:
    A darker, more mature take on the iconic superhero, acclaimed for its storytelling and animation.
  • Sailor Moon:
    Usagi Tsukino transforms into a guardian to fight evil alongside her friends in this magical girl series.
  • Freakazoid!:
    A quirky superhero whose comedic adventures often break the fourth wall and parody pop culture.
  • The Magic School Bus:
    Ms. Frizzle takes her class on educational adventures in a bus that can go anywhere, even inside the human body!

Best Cartoon Villains of All Time

From scheming to diabolical, here are the best villains in cartoon history.

Some of the most iconic villains include:

  • Dr. Robotnik:
    The arch-nemesis of Sonic the Hedgehog, known for his obsession with capturing Sonic and conquering the world.
  • Shredder:
    The primary antagonist of the Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles, a master of ninjutsu and leader of the Foot Clan.
  • Carmen Sandiego:
    A cunning thief who travels the world, known for her elusive nature and intelligence.
  • Vicky:
    The manipulative babysitter from The Fairly OddParents, known for her cruel antics towards Timmy Turner.
  • Plankton:
    The tiny villain from SpongeBob SquarePants who constantly schemes to steal the Krabby Patty secret formula.
